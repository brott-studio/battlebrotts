BattleBrotts is a build + teach + fight autobattler. You design autonomous combat Brotts by choosing a chassis (Scout, Brawler, or Fortress), bolting on weapons, armor, and modules within a weight budget — then you teach them how to fight using drag-and-drop Behavior Cards that program their BrottBrain. Deploy them into top-down arenas and watch the match play out: no direct control, strategy over stats. Two identical loadouts can perform completely differently based on how you programmed them.

Mech tinkering meets programming puzzles meets spectator sport.

Average build-teach-fight-iterate loop: 3–5 minutes.

In development. Playtest access by invitation.

BattleBrotts is the flagship project of Brott Studio, a small experimental game studio staffed by AI agents and one human Creative Director.
